## Project Overview
RSVP Movies is a leading Indian film production company planning to release a movie
for a **global audience**. To support data-driven decision-making and reduce business
risk, this project analyzes historical movie data from the past three years using
**SQL-based analytics**.

The analysis focuses on identifying **high-performing genres, directors, actors,
and audience engagement patterns** to support strategic planning for a global movie
launch.

---

## Business Problem
Launching a movie for a global audience involves significant investment and risk.
RSVP Movies aims to answer critical business questions such as:
- Which genres have consistent global appeal?
- Which directors and actors deliver reliable performance?
- How do ratings and audience votes indicate global acceptance?
- What historical trends can guide future movie planning?

This project converts historical data into **actionable business insights** using
structured SQL analysis.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze historical movie data using SQL
- Perform multi-table joins across a relational database
- Identify high-performing genres, directors, and actors
- Evaluate movie ratings and audience engagement
- Deliver clear insights and recommendations for stakeholders

## Analytical Approach
The analysis was conducted in structured segments:
- Understanding database schema and relationships
- Combining multiple tables using optimized joins
- Aggregating and filtering data to evaluate performance
- Ranking genres, directors, and actors
- Validating results against expected business outcomes

---

## Key Insights
- Certain genres consistently perform well across regions, indicating strong
  global appeal
- A small group of directors demonstrates consistent high ratings over time
- Audience vote count combined with ratings is a strong indicator of acceptance
- Strategic casting plays a critical role in movie success

---

## Business Recommendations
- Prioritize genres with proven global performance
- Collaborate with directors showing consistent audience approval
- Consider actors with strong engagement metrics
- Use historical ratings and votes as decision-support metrics
- Apply data-driven planning for content and casting strategy
